Zorro

It is intended to add links to information about the Zorro bus, as used in Commodore's Amiga machines and Macro System Development's DraCo.

There were two types of Zorro buses:

  • Zorro 2: Present in all Amiga machines; a partial version of it (slightly faster timing, no DMA) also in the DraCo.

  • Zorro 3: Present in A3000 and A4000 Amiga machines.

Jens Schroder has pages with information on both the Zorro 2 and Zorro 3 busses.
