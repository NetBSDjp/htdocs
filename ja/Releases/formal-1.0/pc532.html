NetBSD/pc532 1.0

NetBSD/pc532 1.0 was the first formal release of NetBSD/pc532.

Phil Nelson was the maintainer of NetBSD/pc532 at the time of the 1.0 release.

Supported Hardware

The minimal configuration for a NetBSD/pc532 1.0 system requires 4M of RAM and about 40M of disk space. For a full installation (including source), at least 8M of RAM and 200M of disk space are recommended.

Devices supported by NetBSD/pc532 1.0 include:

  • 8 serial lines done by 4 scn2681 chips
  • NCR DP8490 SCSI chip (SCSI only)
    • Most SCSI disks work (both hard disks and floppies)
    • A few SCSI tapes work
    • A few SCSI CD-ROMs work

If you're interested in NetBSD/pc532 you should use a more recent version.
