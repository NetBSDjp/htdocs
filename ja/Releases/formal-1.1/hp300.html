NetBSD/hp300 1.1

NetBSD/hp300 1.1 was the second formal release of NetBSD/hp300.

Jason Thorpe was the maintainer of NetBSD/hp300 at the time of the 1.1 release.

Supported Hardware

NetBSD/hp300 1.1 will run on most HP 9000/300 series computers. It has been tested on machines with as little as 7.5M of RAM. A minimal installation, less X and source, will fit on a 120M disk.

Devices supported by NetBSD/hp300 1.1 include:

  • CPUs:
    • 68020-based: 318, 319, 320, 330, and 350
    • 68030-based: 340, 345, 360, 370, 375, and 400
    • 68040-based: 380, 425 and 433
  • Disks:
    • HP-IB/CS80: 7912, 7914, 7933, 7936, 7945, 7957, 7958, 7959, 2200, and 2203
    • SCSI-I, including magneto-optical and CD-ROM
  • Tape drives:
    • Low-density HP-IB/CS80 cartridge: 7914, 7946, and 9144
    • High-density HP-IB/CS80 cartridge: 9145
    • HP-IB/CS80 1/2": 7974A, 7978A/B, 7979A, 7980A, and 7980XC.
    • SCSI: HP DAT and Exabyte
  • RS232 interfaces:
    • 98644 built-in single port (dca)
    • 98642 4-port (dcm)
    • 98638 8-port (dcm)
  • Network interfaces:
    • 98643 built-in and add-on LAN cards
  • Displays:
    • 98544, 98545, and 98547 color and monochrome Topcat
    • 98548, 98549, and 98550 color and monochrome Catseye
    • 98700 and 98710 Gatorbox
    • 98720 and 98721 Renaissance
    • 98730 and 98731 DaVinci
    • A1096A monochrome Hyperion
  • Input devices:
    • General interface supporting all HIL devices: keyboard, 2 and 3 button mice, and ID module
  • Miscellaneous:
    • Battery-backed real-time clock
    • 98625A and 98625B built-in HP-IB interface
    • 98658A built-in SCSI interface
    • Printers and plotters on RS232 and HP-IB
    • SCSI autochanger
If you have a device that's not listed, it might be compatible with something else, or we might have simply forgotten it. If all else fails, maybe you could write a driver!

Supported Boot Options

NetBSD/hp300 1.1 supports booting from SCSI and HP-IB disks (SYS_PBOOT) and via local area network (SYS_NBOOT).

If you're interested in NetBSD/hp300 you should use a more recent version.
