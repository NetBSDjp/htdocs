NetBSD does QuakeWorld! You can now enjoy late night fragfests on your i386 machine with GLX based hardware acceleration (MGA or TNT) and sound! This game binary was compiled on NetBSD by porting the recently released Quake1 source code. You can also see a native QuakeWorld Server running in the bottom right xterm.
