May 2001
2001-05-21: New -current snapshot available
A new -current snapshot based on 2001-05-04 sources is available in the NetBSD/sun3 snapshot area of the ftp server.

2001-05-16: 1.5.1_BETA2 snapshot available
A NetBSD/sun3 1.5.1_BETA2 snapshot, based off of 1.5.1 sources as of 2001-05-10, is now available in the NetBSD/sun3 snapshot area of the ftp server. (snapshot deprecated. NetBSD 1.5.1 was released on July 11th, 2001)
April 2001
2001-04-21: 1.5.1_BETA snapshot available
A NetBSD/sun3 1.5.1_BETA snapshot, based off of 1.5.1 sources as of 2001-04-06, is now available in the NetBSD/sun3 snapshot area of the ftp server. (snapshot deprecated. NetBSD 1.5.1 was released on July 11th, 2001)
February 2001
2001-02-22: NetBSD/sun3 switches to ELF
NetBSD/sun3 is now using ELF as its native object file format in -current. An ELF snapshot is available in the NetBSD/sun3 snapshot area of the ftp server. Also available are instructions for upgrading from this snapshot or from source.
December 2000
2000-12-06: NetBSD 1.5 released
NetBSD 1.5 released with support for 20 architectures. More information is available in the 1.5 release announcement.

November 2000
2000-11-25: NetBSD 1.4.3 released
NetBSD 1.4.3 released with support for 16 architectures. More information is available in the 1.4.3 release announcement.
October 2000
2000-10-30: 1.5_BETA snapshot
A sun3 1.5_BETA snapshot is available in the 1.5_BETA area of the ftp server. (snapshot deprecated. NetBSD 1.5 was released on December 6th, 2000)

2000-10-21: sun3x pmap fix
A long-standing pmap bug which causes "out of space kmem_map" panic on sun3x is now fixed.
July 2000
2000-07-18: First 1.4.3_ALPHA snapshot
The first sun3 1.4.3_ALPHA snapshot, based off of 1.4.3 sources as of 2000-07-08, is now available in the NetBSD/sun3 snapshot area of the ftp server. (snapshot deprecated. NetBSD 1.4.3 was released on November 25th, 2000)
June 2000
2000-06-21: New snapshot available
A new snapshot based on 2000-06-10 sources is available in the NetBSD/sun3 snapshot area of the ftp server.
April 2000
2000-04-29: Binary Packages
Over 600 MB of binary packages are now available for NetBSD/sun3 1.4.2.
March 2000
2000-03-19: NetBSD 1.4.2 released
NetBSD 1.4.2 released with support for 16 architectures.
December 1999
1999-12-09: NetBSD-current snapshot
Based on 1999-12-09 sources, available in the NetBSD/sun3 snapshot area of the ftp server.
June 1999
1999-06-23: New sun3 portmaster
Nathan Williams takes over from Gordon Ross, who was the sun3 port maintainer for several years.
May 1999

The NetBSD 1.4 release supports sixteen architectures, and was distributed free by Usenix to all 1999 ATC attendees.
