The NetBSD Foundation, Inc.

The NetBSD Foundation serves as the legal entity which owns several of the NetBSD Project servers, handles donations of money and hardware to the project, and administers NetBSD copyrights.

The NetBSD Foundation is incorporated in the State of Delaware. It is not currently 501(c)3 (`non-profit').

The NetBSD Foundation is:

  • Charles Hannum <mycroft@netbsd.org>, president and chairman
  • Christos Zoulas <christos@netbsd.org>, director
