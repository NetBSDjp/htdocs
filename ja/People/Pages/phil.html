Phil Nelson

My major involvement with NetBSD is that I did most of the kernel work for the NetBSD/pc532 port and I am the port maintainer for that port. I also helped get this WWW server up and working. I currently maintain scripts to keep this site synced with the CVS repository.

Steelhead

Steelhead is my pc532 and is the machine on which most of the NetBSD/pc532 development was done. It was the first machine to run NetBSD/pc532!

I also use NetBSD! I manage a small laboratory of machines that uses the NetBSD/arm32 port.
