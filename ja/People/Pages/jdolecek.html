My involvement with the NetBSD Project

I came to NetBSD in late 1.1, about a month before 1.2 release. It was time when I purchased my own computer and one of collegues suggested trying out NetBSD - and doing so was one of my good decisions :)
I've become a developer on July 21st 1999. I care of NTFS (with general FS hacking from time to time). I'm also going to actually integrate Scott Telford's and McGarry's MCA patches - the integration should happen sometimes after 1.5 release.
I'd also like to see better support for iso-8859-2 and czech language in general (sysinst, libc); however, that work is rather low-priority one.

RL

I live in Brno in Czech Republic. I like biking a lot and some hiking from time to time.

I worked for Institute of Computer Science of Masaryk university in the past while I was on the university. Now, I work in small software company Per4mance, some DB staff involving Oracle 8i. I'd like to find a job where I could do some more NetBSD hacking though *daydream* ;-)
