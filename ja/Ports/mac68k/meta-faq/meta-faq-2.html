2. Obtaining NetBSD/mac68k

Contents of this section

The primary method for obtaining NetBSD/mac68k is by anonymous ftp. The best location for obtaining the 1.3 release of NetBSD/mac68k is probably from NetBSD.ORG or one of its mirrors:

ftp://ftp.netbsd.org/pub/NetBSD/NetBSD-1.3/mac68k/

A more or less complete list of ftp mirror sites is available via the WWW at: http://www.netbsd.org/Sites/
or by anonymous ftp in the file: ftp://ftp.netbsd.org/pub/NetBSD/NetBSD-current/doc/MIRRORS
