5. Recommended reading

Contents of this section

5.1 UNIX and BSD information

The following books are recommended as sources of information about UNIX operating systems:

  • Title: UNIX System Administration Handbook
    Author: Evi Nemeth, Garth Snyder and Scott Seebass
    Publication: Prentice-Hall, 2nd edition, 1994
  • Title: Advanced Programming in the Unix Environment
    Author: W. Richard Stevens
    Publication:
  • Title: The Design and Implementation of the 4.4 BSD Operating System
    Author: Marshall Kirk McKusick, Keith Bostic, Michael J. Karels, John S. Quarterman
    Publication: Addison Wesley, 1996
  • Title: Unix Internals: The New Frontiers
    Author: Uresh Vahalia
    Publication:

5.2 Macintosh information

The following books are good sources of information on Macintosh hardware and devices:

  • Title: Guide to the Macintosh Family Hardware
    Author: Apple Computer, Inc.
    Publication: Addison-Wesley, 2nd Edition, 1990
  • Title: Designing Cards and Drivers for the Macintosh Family
    Author: Apple Computer, Inc.
    Publication: Addison-Wesley, 3rd Edition, 1992
