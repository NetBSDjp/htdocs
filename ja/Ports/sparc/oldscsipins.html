Old SCSI to Normal SCSI pins

The convention used here is that the normal 50 pin, two-row cable starts at pin 1 on the upper left of the connector and goes to pin 25 before wrapping around: 

 1 ... 25
26 ... 50
and the old style three-row connector starts at pin 1 and goes to pin 17 before wrapping around: 
 1 ... 17
18 ... 33
34 ... 50
As usual, use this with the standard disclaimers, no warranty applies.
