NetBSD/algor news archive

March 2004
2004-03-01: NetBSD 1.6.2 released
NetBSD 1.6.2 released with support for 40 architectures. More information is available in the 1.6.2 release announcement.
April 2003
2003-04-21: NetBSD 1.6.1 released
NetBSD 1.6.1 released with support for 40 architectures. More information is available in the 1.6.1 release announcement.
September 2002
2002-09-14: NetBSD 1.6 released
NetBSD 1.6 released with support for 39 architectures. More information is available in the 1.6 release announcement.
June 2001
2001-06-01: Support for Algorithmics P-4032 board committed
Jason Thorpe has written and committed support for the Algorithmics P-4032, an older board for the R4xxx CPU family. Thanks to Carl Shapiro for providing a P-4032 board.
May 2001
2001-05-28: NetBSD/algor port committed
The NetBSD/algor port is committed to the source tree. The port initially supports the Algorithmics P-5064 board. The port was done by Jason Thorpe, with a P-5064 board provided by Allegro Networks.
