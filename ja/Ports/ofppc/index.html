NetBSD/ofppc

About NetBSD/ofppc 

NetBSD/ofppc is the port of NetBSD to the OpenFirmware PowerPC machine, and original implementation of NetBSD for the PowerPC processor.

Most functions of NetBSD/ofppc kernel can be simulated by PSIM (PowerPC Simulator). See NetBSD/ofppc with PSIM HOWTO!
