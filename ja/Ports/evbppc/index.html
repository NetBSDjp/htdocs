NetBSD/evbppc

About NetBSD/evbppc 

NetBSD/evbppc is intended to be a port of NetBSD to various PowerPC-based evaluation boards.

At this time, the IBM PowerPC 405GP based walnut evaluation board is supported by NetBSD/evbppc. More boards will be added in due course.
