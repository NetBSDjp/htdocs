NetBSD/i386 history

NetBSD/i386 was the original port of NetBSD, and was initially released as NetBSD 0.8. There were complete sets of binaries made of NetBSD/i386 for the NetBSD 0.9, NetBSD 1.0, NetBSD 1.1, NetBSD 1.2 and NetBSD 1.3 releases.
