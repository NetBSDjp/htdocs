NetBSD/i386 History

NetBSD/i386 was the original port of NetBSD, and was initially released as NetBSD 0.8. There were complete sets of binaries made of NetBSD/i386 for the NetBSD 0.9, NetBSD 1.0, NetBSD 1.1, NetBSD 1.2, NetBSD 1.3, NetBSD 1.4, NetBSD 1.5 and NetBSD 1.6 releases.

Frank van der Linden is the current port maintainer of NetBSD/i386.
