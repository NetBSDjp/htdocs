September 2002
2002-09-05: New -current kernel available
A new kernel, bootloader, and ld.elf_so have been uploaded. These fix problems from the snapshot based on 2002-07-06 sources. Copy the files from the hp700-update-20020905.tar.gz to your system.

This is the first snapshot with shared libraries.
