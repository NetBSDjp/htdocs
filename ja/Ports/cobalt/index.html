NetBSD/cobalt

About NetBSD/cobalt 

NetBSD/cobalt is the port of NetBSD to Cobalt Networks' MIPS-based Microservers, now known as Sun Server Appliances.

Supported System Models 

All MIPS-based Qube and RaQ series systems from Cobalt Networks as well as the Gateway Micro Servers are supported.

The Phoenix Firewall Appliance Microserver and some versions of the Seagate NasRaQ may also be supported.

A built-in serial port is required for installation.


RaQ 3/4 Note 

Note that the i386-based RaQ 3, RaQ 3i and RaQ 4 models are not supported by the cobalt port.
