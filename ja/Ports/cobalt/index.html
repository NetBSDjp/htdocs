About NetBSD/cobalt

NetBSD/cobalt is the port of NetBSD to Cobalt Networks' Microservers.

Supported System Models
All MIPS-based Qube and RaQ series systems from Cobalt Networks as well as the Gateway Micro Servers are supported. The Phoenix Firewall Appliance Microserver may also be supported.

A built-in serial port is required for installation.

RaQ 3/4 Note
Note that the i386-based RaQ 3, RaQ 3i and RaQ 4 models are not supported by the cobalt port.
