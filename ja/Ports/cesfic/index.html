NetBSD/cesfic

NetBSD/cesfic is a new port of NetBSD to the m68040 based FIC8234 VME processor board, made by the swiss company CES (Geneve). These boards are (or have been) popular in high energy physics data acquisition (think of CERN!). See http://www.ces.ch/Products/CPUs/FIC8234/FIC8234.html for some technical data. More details will show up here shortly.
