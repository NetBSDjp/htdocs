NetBSD/openblocks

About NetBSD/openblocks 

NetBSD/openblocks is a port to the OpenBlockS PowerPC 860 based micro server, made by Plat'Home CO., LTD.

Takashi Hosokawa is the port maintainer of NetBSD/openblocks.


NetBSD/openblocks News 

NetBSD/openblocks has not yet been integrated with the NetBSD source tree. It will be shortly.
