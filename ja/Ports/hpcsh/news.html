NetBSD/hpcsh news archive

March 2004
2004-03-01: NetBSD 1.6.2 released
NetBSD 1.6.2 released with support for 40 architectures. More information is available in the 1.6.2 release announcement.
October 2003
2003-10-19: Driver for HP Jornada 680/690 touch-screen
Support for HP Jornada 680/690 touch-screen is now provided by the j6x0tp(4) driver. See the post by Valeriy Ushakov for more information.
June 2003
2003-06-05: HP Jornada Palmtop NetBSD article published
O'Reilly ONLamp has published an article by Michael Lucas, Palmtop NetBSD, covering the installation of NetBSD on a HP Jornada 728. It may also be of interest to hpcsh users.
