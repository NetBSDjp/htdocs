NetBSD/sh3

NetBSD supports Hitachi Super-H family CPUs in the following ports:

  • mmeye: Brains mmEye
  • evbsh3: SH3 chip evaluation boards
    • CqREEK-SH3 (CQ Publishing)
    • 7709A evaluation board (Computex)
  • evbsh3: SH4 chip evaluation boards
    • KZ-SH4-01 ( Kyoto Micro Computer Co. LTD)
    • CqREEK-SH4 (CQ Publishing, same hardware as KMC KZ-SH4-01)
    • 7750 evaluation board (Computex)
  • dreamcast: Sega Dreamcast
  • hpcsh: SH3/4-based WinCE machines
