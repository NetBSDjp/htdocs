NetBSD/arm32 devices supported on Acorn machines

On-board hardware

Most standard Acorn hardware is catered for. This includes VIDC (video console), IOMD (general purpose IO), serial port, parallel port, mouse (both quadrature and PS/2), internal IDE interface, floppy disc and keyboard.

Ethernet interfaces

NetBSD/arm32 supports:

RiscOS driver name Manufacturer NetBSD driver name
Ether1Acornie0
Ether3ANTea0
Ether5ANTea0
EtherBANTeb0
EtherH NICI-cubedne0
EtherM NICANTne0

EtherH and EtherM NIC drivers are presently the best performers.

IO cards

The following IO cards are supported:

  • Acorn SCSI
  • Cumana SCSI I and II
  • Alsystems Powertec SCSI II (maybe/apparently SCSI III)
  • MCS Connect32
  • Oak SCSI
  • Morley uncached SCSI
  • Yellowstone RapIDE (issue 2)
  • Simtec low-cost IDE
  • ICS IDE Arcin v5 and v6

The speed of the SCSI drivers is presently relatevely poor, whereas the IDE drivers perform well.
