NetBSD/arm32 on-line resources

The mailing list

A mailing list is provided by the NetBSD Foundation which provides for the discussion of all matters related to the arm32 port and RiscBSD distribution. To join the mailing list, send an e-mail message to majordomo@NetBSD.org with the text subscribe port-arm32 as a single line in the message body.

An archive of all NetBSD.org mailing lists is available, as are mailing lists specific to other ports and various areas of the operating system in general. For more information on NetBSD.org mailing lists, a dedicated mailing lists page exists.

Shark-specific information

For information related to the Shark hardware, have a look at the Internet Appliance Group web site. Todd Vierling has also created a mailing list specific to Shark users and developers together with a collection of other bits and bobs. His pages are presently at http://www.duh.org/shark/.

The 'port master.'

Presently, the port master of the NetBSD/arm32 port is Mark Brinicombe. He can be reached by e-mailing Mark@NetBSD.org.
