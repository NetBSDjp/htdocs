NetBSD/pmppc

About NetBSD/pmppc 

NetBSD/pmppc is the port of NetBSD to Artesyn's PM/PPC board.


NetBSD/pmppc News 

2002-05-31: NetBSD ported to Artesyns' PM/PPC board.
NetBSD/pmppc is currently able to boot to multi-user as a diskless NFS client.


Supported Hardware 

Supported:

  • network
  • serial ports
  • PCI bridge
  • real time clock

Unsupported:

  • flash
  • IIC
  • ROM
