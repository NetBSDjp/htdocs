Brains Corporation

Brains Corporation

Brains Corporation is based in Tokyo, Japan, setup in 1985.

NetBSD/sh3 port

Brains financed a port of NetBSD 1.3.1 to their Hitachi Super-H 3 based mmEye product, then very generously made their diffs publically available, and assisted with the importation of NetBSD/sh3 into the main NetBSD tree.

mmEye hardware contribution

Brains also shipped an mmEye unit to the NetBSD foundation for display in the Comdex 1999 exhibition.
