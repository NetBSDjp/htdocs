SMC Networks Inc.

SMC have provided the following information regarding their network products:

SMC Networks, Inc. is a leading solutions provider of network products engineered to increase the performance and productivity of small-to-medium size organizations. There are two distinct product lines designed to fulfill our customer's specific needs.

The EZ Line focuses on inexpensive, easy to use desktop products while the Tiger Line features high-performance managed workgroup products. Supplementing these products is a comprehensive support package includes a strong warranty (lifetime in most instances), superior customer service, free cross-shipment and free technical support. NetBSD supports the following SMC products:

SMC EtherPower II Model: SMC9432 Series
SMC EtherPower 10/100 Model: SMC9332BDT
SMC EtherPower Model: SMC8432 Series
SMC EtherPower (2) Model: SMC9334BDT
SMC EZ Card 10 Model: SMC1660 (ISA) Series
SMC EZ Card 10 Model: SMC1208 (PCI) Series
SMC PC Card 10 Model: SMC8022 (PCMCIA)
SMC PC Card 10/100 Model: SMC8034TX (Card Bus PCMCIA)

For more information please see the web site http://www.smc.com or send e-mail to <techsupport@smc.com>.
