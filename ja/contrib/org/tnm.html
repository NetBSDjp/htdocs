The NetBSD Mission

The NetBSD Mission exists to provide staff, literature, promotional items, software distributions, and funding for miscellaneous expenses, both at trade shows and over the web.

Some of the events the Mission has supported include:

  • Usenix Annual Technical Conference 2001
  • Comdex Spring 2001
  • LinuxWorld Expo
  • Comdex Fall 2000
  • Linux Expo Toronto 2000
  • Atlanta Linux Showcase 2000
  • O'Reilly Open Source Software Convention 2000
  • Comdex Canada 2000
  • Usenix Annual Technical Conference 2000
  • Comdex Spring 2000
  • Linux Expo Montreal 2000
  • Linux Expo Paris 2000
  • The Bazaar
  • Comdex Fall 1999
  • Atlanta Linux Showcase 1999

 The Mission is primarily funded with consulting revenue from Charles Hannum, but external contributions are welcome.
