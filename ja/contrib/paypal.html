Donating money to NetBSD

PayPal is essentially an online bank that allows its users to easily transfer money among each other. Once you have an account you can transfer money by entering the email address of the person or organization you want to send it to. The PayPal address for NetBSD is paypal@netbsd.org. The PayPal site will guide you through all the steps you need to take to open an account and send money.

To donate using PayPal, start here:

If you have any questions or if you don't want your name to be added to the list of contributors, please let us know at <paypal@netbsd.org>.

If you want to make a monetary donation but prefer not to use PayPal, or if you want to make custom arrangements of some sort, please contact the NetBSD treasurer at <accounts@netbsd.org>.
