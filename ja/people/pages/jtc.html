I probably am best characterized as the standards-weenie of the NetBSD Core Group. I do my best to make the programming and user environment provided by NetBSD conform to reasonable industry standards. Reasonable is the key word, X/Open 1170 is not reasonable.

In real life, I work at Cygnus Support, which provides commercial support for Sourceware solutions including software development tools and Kerberos-based security products.
