Welcome to the NetBSD Project
``Of course it runs NetBSD.''

The NetBSD Project is a collective volunteer effort to produce a freely available and redistributable UNIX-like operating system.

NetBSD runs on a broad range of hardware platforms and is highly portable. It comes with complete source code, and is user-supported.
