Miscellaneous

Listed here are links to miscellaneous hardware information that NetBSD users and hackers might find interesting. If you find a link that could be handy, please let us know!

  • NetBSD Serial Port Primer
    An introduction to connecting serial ports. Also includes information on the pinouts of some of the supported platforms
  • Tom's hardware guide and Tom's Networking
    Motherboard, CPU, chipset, RAM, disk, video, BIOS, and overclocking guides as well as all things networking. For the Intel compatible user...
  • Tommy's pinout collection
    Pinouts for various comms, console, video, disk, analog, and bus interfaces.
  • comp.dcom.lans.ethernet FAQs
    Ethernet Network Questions and Answers.
